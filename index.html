Mathematical Properties

24 is a highly composite number with 8 divisors: 1, 2, 3, 4, 6, 8, 12, and 24. It's also a factorial: 4! = 24.

24 is also the atomic number of chromium, and there are 24 major and minor keys in Western music (12 major and 12 minor).
